What is BiPartisan?
BiPartisan is an app that lets you view news in a meaningful way by discovering the most truthful news from the right places.
What Can I Do?
Want news in a certain place? Check out our NewStream, a live map of the world. Click anywhere to see the top news in that location right now. News is organized by topic as well as our BiScore — a score that shows how truthful and accurate a certain article is.
Have a specific article that you want us to analyze? Head over to the BiScore tab to enter the link to any article you stumble upon and get our official confidence rating in that article.
Don't have a specific article? Just search for a topic of choice in the search bar at the top!
